Corporate

The Company’s management / key personnel team has extensive experience in the mining industry and, in particular, with publicly traded junior mineral exploration companies.

Lori McClenahan

Director, President, and CEO of the Company, has extensive legal, financing and administrative experience with junior resource companies. Ms. McClenahan was formerly a stockbroker and a corporate finance associate with C.M. Oliver & Company Limited.

Kulwant Sandher

Chief Financial Officer of the Company, has over 19 years of international financial management experience in both the public and private company sectors.

Paul McDonald

Director, brings to Intigold Mines Ltd over 40 years of diverse and valuable experience in financing and development of growth companies in the resource sector. He was previously VP of the Montreal Stock Exchange and has had numerous appointments to corporate boards. He has a law degree from McGill University.

Paul Gray

Director, is the principal of Paul D. Gray Geological Consultants. He handles Northern Canada exploration projects from the permitting through execution. Mr. Gray sits on the Boards of Argus Metals Corp, Dawson Gold Corp., GeoNovus Minerals Corp. and Tajiri Resources Corp. Paul Grey holds a Bachelor of Science (Honours) degree from Dalhousie University and is a member in good standing with the Association of Professional Engineers and Geoscientists of British Columbia. Mr. Gray has worked extensively as an exploration geologist in the Mineral Exploration Industry in Canada, the United States, Asia and Central and South America for the past 17 years, concentrating on precious metals, base metals and uranium exploration and development. Mr. Gray has been working in the Yukon and North West Territories with advanced gold exploration projects since 2004 and has more than 3 years directly on the Hyland Gold Project where he designed and managed Argus’ exploration efforts and additionally, Mr. Gray served as President and COO of Doublestar Resources Ltd. until it was acquired by Selkirk Metals Corp. in July of 2007.
