The Property:

The Gold Post property is located in the Osoyoos Mining Division, of south-central British Columbia. The property covers 4663 hectares (46.63 km2). The property covers 1 known zone of silver-lead-zinc and gold mineralization. The Gold Post Project is located adjacent to the Orofino Mountain gold camp an area with numerous known mineral occurrences and past producers. The Orofino Mountain gold camp is host to gold in quartz veins.


Intigold is currently carrying out its 2012 exploration program.
